Rafting GT

Each rapid brought cheers and laughter, while the calmer stretches revealed colorful birds and playful monkeys. This rafting adventure left me feeling alive and deeply appreciative of Guatemala's natural beauty.

History

Guatemala is a premier rafting destination, gaining international attention in the last years with the rise of adventure tourism. Some areas are indigenous Maya communities that have historically utilized the rivers, adding cultural depth to the experience.

Commercial rafting companies have made Guatemala accessible to a wider audience, boosting the local economy through tourism. Guatemala counts Class IV and V rapids attract thrill-seekers, making this great adventure a must-visit for rafting enthusiasts.
